About Us

Who are we

Hedmenn Strauss (Pty) Ltd was established in 2006, with a vision to be a financial services provider for sustainable energy solutions. We develop projects that fulfill a specific client need, through collaborative efforts with them using mature software technonologies from leading industry providers as well as technical expertise from Original Equipment Manufacturers; Engineering Service Providers and Financial Institutions. A problem solving approach that works, for a sustainable energy future.

What is Project Development

The effort to establish energy needs; and create an enabling environment often involves hidden costs ; misaligned technical outcomes and a lot of regulation. Our project development process spans over 12 years of experience, with established expertise in putting a project together. Hedmenn’s specific area of expertise is Photovoltaic as well as Biomass Technology. The industry is rapidly changing, with Hydrogen and Battery Energy Storage now becoming a key development area, while incorporating the former Technologies in their development.

How integral is Financial Services to Project Development

Maturity of the sustainable energy industry has led to much easier determinations of a project’s viability, which brings to fore the next hurdke of capital investment, in both the development process as well as the construction process. Our experience in the former as a licensed Venture Capital Company, and the latter as authorised intermediary of Project and Commercial Insurance products, allowing us to offer bancassurance services.

Where does Credit Provision feature in Project Development

When Project Developers requires only working capital to service markets such as Commercial and Industrial clients. A combination of products such as financial guarantees and credit guarantees can sometimes be the difference between a planned project and an executed project, as it can facilitate short-term credit facilities through financial institutions. Hedmenns is an intermediary for a panel of underwriters offering such products, as well as those during the execution phase.
