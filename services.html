Our Services

Construction All-Risk Cover

This cover lasts for the duration of the project implementation stage and may include a warranty period. It requires land evaluation, environmental assessment, feasibility studies, and geotechnical contour analysis.

Marine Insurance

Many components are sourced internationally and require shipping, clearing, transit to site, and storage protection. Detailed descriptions and insurance support are submitted with financing conditions.

Financial Guarantees

Contractors may need to provide performance bonds or guarantees to project developers or banks. Guarantees may also be required for long-term obligations under power purchase agreements.

Credit Insurance

This product is recently regaining favour where a supplier of goods insures the ability of any client to pay them over a shorter period, usually six months. This enables a supplier to extend more products on credit and not lose market share by internally carrying credit risk on their books.

Commercial Insurance

Operational risk coverage including Business Interruption and standard commercial insurance required once a project is fully operational.

Nuclear Strict Liability Insurance

Compliance with the NNR Act 47 of 1999, including nuclear liability securities and extended term provisions, requiring specialized coverage for nuclear installations.
